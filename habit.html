How to Build a Spiritual Reading Habit?

Follow the following steps really to improve your habit of reading Spiritual Books

Step by Step Guide:

Step 1:Start in small

Start with small length of time(10-15 minutes) per day.Don't try to cover too much in once.Everything is little by little.

Step 2:Choose the right time to read

Pick a specific time , it can be in the morning or any other time . But I highly recommend to read in the morning, since our mind is very clear with no distractions.

Step 3:Find a quiet place to read

Read in peaceful and clear environment. This is because it helps you to give more focus on what are doing , and this increases concentrating on your reading.

Step 4:Consistency

Consistency is the powerful thing you need to apply in your plan. So , try to read everyday and move with improvements.

Step 5:Reflect

Think about what you read and practice it in your life,since the main objective is to improve yourself with reading and lead your life with right ideas.

Daily Routine

  • Morning read for 10-15 minutes minimum
  • Reflect on what you read, just to try to memorize it
  • Read a small section in the night before you sleep

Common challenges

I don't have time

Small time is enough to begin,just try to invest for few minutes daily. If you act wtih plan, time is more than enough, so don't excuse for such questios of mind.

I forgot to Read

This can also a challenge so try to set a daily reminder not to forget your new habit.This can be Alarm or other method.

I lose motivation

You can feel boring and tired to read,but read inspiring passages and remember your purpose.Building good resilience can break these weak assumptions.

"Consistency is more important than quantity.A small daily habit builds a strong spiritual life."
