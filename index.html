Orthodox Spiritual Reading Habit

Build a daily habit of reading spiritual books for wisdom, and inner growth.

This website is designed to help people build a daily habit of reading Orthodox spiritual books. It emphasizes consistency, inner peace, and spiritual growth, rather than occasional reading.I highly encourage everyone to develop the habit of reading spiritual books in order to experience real transformation in life. Reading spiritual books is very different from reading ordinary, worldly books. This is because they carry a deeper purpose and meaning. I truly believe that you will be greatly blessed through them, because these words are the words of God. They have the power to cleanse the mind, remove negative and sinful thoughts, and guide a person toward a more peaceful and faithful life.

why Spiritual Reading Habits Matter

  • Strengthens Spiritual life
  • Improves Discipline and Consistency
  • Builds your life according to God's rules
  • Brings Peace of Mind
  • Builds Focus and thinking power

How to Start the Habit?

  1. Start reading 10-15 minutes per day
  2. Begin with simple and attractive books
  3. Choose quiet and clean place to read
  4. Move based on your plan and Be consistent everyday
  5. Reflect on what you read
